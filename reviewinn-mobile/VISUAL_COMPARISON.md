### After (Efficient)
```sql

  r.*,
  (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM comments WHERE review_id = r.id) as comments_count
FROM reviews r
WHERE r.id = 123;

-- âœ… Step 2: Load comments separately (WHEN NEEDED)

  c.*,
  u.username,
  u.avatar
FROM comments c
LEFT JOIN users u ON u.id = c.user_id

ORDER BY c.created_at DESC
LIMIT 20 OFFSET 0;

-- Benefits:
-- âœ… Single row for review (super fast)
```
